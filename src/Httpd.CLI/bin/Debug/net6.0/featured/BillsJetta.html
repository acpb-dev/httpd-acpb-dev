Bill's MK4 Jetta

 

This is Bill's 2001 Jetta 1.8T. He's had it for about 6 months now and enjoyed almost every minute with it. It has a bigger turbo (K04), injectors, intercooler and has Unitronic's Stage 2+ Tune good for 265hp and 300lb-ft The exterior is pretty much stock which makes it a great sleeper. It wasn't the most reliable car, however it's mostly due to the car's age and kilometers.

The projects coming for this car are a full body restoration and respray next summer, new suspension components since the ride quality is starting to get a bit rough. Other than that he won't touch the engine exept regular maintenance. He's currently shopping for a new MK4 VW and dumping money into this one isn't logical right now.
